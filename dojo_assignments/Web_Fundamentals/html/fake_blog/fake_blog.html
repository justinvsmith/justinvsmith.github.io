Bay Area Donuts!

Who doesn't love a good donut? They're the best treat to have any time of the day. Although you shouldn't have them too frequently, once in a while it's a great treat to be able to have a donut, or even a donut hole! Here are some great places in the San Francisco Bay area, to get yourself a donut.

Name Location Address Rating
Bob's Donuts San Francisco 1621 Polk Street 4.5 out of 5 stars
Donut Savant Oakland 1934 Broadway 4.5 out of 5 stars
Pepple's Donut Farm Oakland 6037 San Pablo Ave. 4 out of 5 stars
Psycho Donuts South Bay/Peninsula 2006 S. Winchester Blvd. 3 out of 5 stars
Johnny Doughnuts North Bay 1617 4th St. 4 out of 5 stars

Interesting facts:

  • Bob's Donuts has a donut challenge (eating a huge donut in one sitting)!
  • Donut Savant has cronuts (mix of donut and croissant).
  • Donut farm is entirely organic and vegan!
  • Psycho Donuts has crazy creations (like a donut topped with strawberry Pocky)!
